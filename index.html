Click the button below to chat with our health and fitness bot. Get instant answers to your health-related questions and receive personalized advice to help you overcome your issues and achieve your wellness goals.

Core Features

Symptom Checker

Users can describe symptoms, and the chatbot provides general advice, suggests lifestyle changes, or recommends seeing a doctor.

Daily Health Tips

Push short health tips, such as staying hydrated, practicing mindfulness, and recommended exercises.

Personalized Exercise Plans

Based on user inputs like age, weight, health goals, and any injuries or conditions.

Diet and Nutrition Advice

Offer dietary suggestions, meal plans, and recipes based on users’ dietary preferences, goals, and restrictions.

Mental Wellness Support

Include exercises for mental health, such as breathing techniques, meditation guides, or self-care reminders.

Routine Tracker

Enable users to log their workouts, water intake, sleep, and other health habits.

Emergency Advice

Provide first-aid tips for common injuries, and make sure users are aware that it’s not a replacement for professional medical advice.

Reminders and Alerts

Notify users to take breaks, drink water, or exercise based on their preferences.

Fitness Workouts
